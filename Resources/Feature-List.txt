Feature List:

- A procedurally generated dungeon filled with traps, puzzles, and enemies
- Over 50 different classes and races to choose from, each with their own strengths and weaknesses
- Hundreds of different weapons, armor, spells, and items to discover and use
- Complex game mechanics including hunger, encumbrance, and item identification
- Dynamic and emergent gameplay that encourages creative problem-solving and experimentation
- A variety of different game modes, including a traditional roguelike mode and a more accessible variant mode
- Permadeath and the ability to save and load games for a truly challenging and rewarding experience
- Multiple paths to victory, with different endings and outcomes depending on the player's choices and actions
- A vibrant modding community with a wide variety of user-created content available to download and play
- Multiplayer support for up to four players, with co-op and competitive play modes
